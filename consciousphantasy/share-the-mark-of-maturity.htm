I. Introduction
Dân hai lăm triệu ai người lớn.
Nước bốn ngàn năm vẫn trẻ con.
(傘沱)
Above verses mean that "Vietnamese are still immature even though the country has a cultural of 4.000 years old". 潘周楨, a Vietnamese though about ethics and moral. He claims that Vietnam should follow democracy policy which also is similar to Chinese 孟子 though (a school of Confucianism). 孟子 claims that 民為貴，社稷次之，君為輕 which means that people is most important then country and finally the king of country. He expects that Vietnamese should be conservative, keep their traditional moral which base on Confucianism beliefs and values. There are five virtues should be archived:
Rén (仁, benevolence, humaneness);
Yì (義, righteousness or justice);
Lǐ (禮, proper rite)
Zhì (智, knowledge)
Xìn (信, integrity)
Vietnamese are still immature because they depend to the king or a leader to follow ethics but not build their own moral. He claims that ethics may be chanced but the moral is constant, and Vietnamese does not build this moral well. A mature person should possess above five virtues. And the marks of maturity are:
Person's behaviors should follow ethics not person's desire.
Understanding genuine king (天命) and live as genuine king expectation (this should be understanding as another sense or meaning because of the age: "He/she should have skills and knowledge to do right things".)
For immaturity marks, Vietnamese did not know, at that time (in the past):
"What is their country?". He claims that people should know what is their country and love their country.
"Who they are?", He claims that children should not be their parent assets. They should have their own though and beliefs.
II. Modern opinions
1. Dr. Lisa Damour at TEDxCLE
Maturity marks:
Risk assessment:
Immature person: Assess risk in the term of the chances of getting caught engaging in risky behavior. For example: What will happen if I do not stop at red traffic light? Is there a risk that I am caught by a police.
Mature person: Assess risk in the term of the chances of getting caught engaging in risky behavior. For example: What will happen if I do not stop at red traffic light? Is there a risk that an accident will happen?
Crazy spot management:
Immature person: could not management crazy spots.
Mature person: could management crazy spots.
Formal operations:
Immature person: could not do abstract reasoning to think about thinking.
Mature person: could do abstract reasoning to think about thinking.
Goal orientation:
Immature person: pursue interested things, could not deal with obstacles.
Mature person: Make and pursue meaningful goals and could deal with obstacles.
2. Psychologytoday
Maturity marks:
A mature person is able to keep long-term commitments.
A mature person is unshaken by flattery or criticism.
A mature person possesses a spirit of humility.
A mature person’s decisions are based on character, not feelings.
A mature person expresses gratitude consistently.
A mature person knows how to prioritize others before themselves.
A mature person seeks wisdom before acting.
3. Karl Marx
Opinions:
A person's personality has two aspects:
Human species: physiological aspects of person's personality.
Human being: social aspects of person's personality. This aspect is important and learned via social activities, marjorly based on working and practice.
Maturity marks: human being, the social aspects of person's personality is developed to a state that:
Have enough skills to work effects.
Have enough skills to behave as social expectation.
