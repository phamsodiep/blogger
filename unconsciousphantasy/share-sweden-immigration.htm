I. Mandatory note
There are some major difference things from my Vietnam country that I think it should be noted here so that the trip is not stuck.
1. Payment
Payment in Sweden is cashless, so you must have a bank card for at least Taxi travel purpose. Visa gift debit card which is issued in your country could be used for taxi, but I do not know if it could be used for other services. It seems that there is a Bank at the airport so that you could create a bank account with USD or EUR cash. SEK is most recommended. There is another alternative payment method of bank card is mobile payment application (anyway, a bank account is always need).
2. Cultural
Language: It seems that English could be used in Sweden for many services communication.
Food: Sweden food is not same with Asia or Vietnamese. It seems that there is not so much carbohydrate food, so for rice substitute, bread may be a choice, but it is not common and may be should be buy at another bakery instead of restaurant or canteen. For my case, I am afraid of hungry because of carbohydrate starvation.
